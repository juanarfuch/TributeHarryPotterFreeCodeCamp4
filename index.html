Harry Potter

The boy who lived

Here's a timeline of Harry Potter's life:

  • 31 July 1980: Harry James Potter is born at St. Mungo's Hospital in London.
  • 31 October 1981: The deaths of Harry's parents, James and Lily Potter, occur at the hands of Lord Voldemort. Harry survives the attack and is left with a lightning-shaped scar on his forehead.
  • 1991: Harry receives a letter from Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, informing him that he is a wizard. He begins his education at Hogwarts and becomes fast friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.
  • 1992: Harry and his friends uncover the existence of the Philosopher's Stone and protect it from Voldemort.
  • 1993: Harry and Hermione use a Time-Turner to travel back in time and save Sirius Black, who is revealed to be Harry's godfather.
  • 1994: Harry is chosen to compete in the Triwizard Tournament and discovers that Voldemort has returned.
  • 1995: Harry forms Dumbledore's Army, a secret organisation dedicated to teaching students Defence Against the Dark Arts, in response to Dolores Umbridge's oppressive regime.
  • 1996: Harry discovers a prophecy that states he is the only one who can defeat Voldemort. He and his friends embark on a mission to destroy Voldemort's Horcruxes, objects that contain fragments of his soul.
  • 1998: Harry and his friends participate in the Battle of Hogwarts, where they successfully defeat Voldemort and his followers.
  • 2017: Harry is married to Ginny Weasley and is a father of three children: James Sirius, Albus Severus, and Lily Luna. He is employed as the head of the Auror Office at the Ministry of Magic.
